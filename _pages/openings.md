### For MDs who would like to do a PhD
If you are an MD, or are about to complete your MD degree, have a look at the [Elmezzi Graduate School](https://www.northwell.edu/education-and-resources/elmezzi-graduate-school-of-molecular-medicine) at The Feinstein Institutes. It is a fantastic program for clinicians to obtain a PhD and work with us or other labs at the Feinstein. It requires minimal course work, is tuiton-free, provides a competitive salary, and is open to international graduates. Email [me (Stephan)](mailto:sbickel@northwell.edu) if you are interested.
